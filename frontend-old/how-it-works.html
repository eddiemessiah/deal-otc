Turning Weeks into Days

Traditional institutional crypto onboarding takes 2-4 weeks. Family offices, corporate treasuries, and hedge funds get bogged down in email chains, PDF shuffling, and manual compliance verifications across different global jurisdictions.

Deal OTC Automation Infrastructure eliminates this friction. By leveraging determinisitic AI, RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) compliance engines, and automated CRM tracking, we reduce time-to-trade to under 48 hours.

The Automated Pipeline

STEP 1: Document Ingestion

Clients drop their Mandate Letters, Proof of Funds (PoF), and corporate registries into the Deal OTC Terminal or via secure Telegram. Our AI Clause Extractor immediately parses the PDFs, pulling out specific UBOs (Ultimate Beneficial Owners), jurisdictions, and capital limits.

STEP 2: The Compliance RAG Engine

The extracted data is cross-referenced against the strict Deal OTC 3-Tier Walled Garden guidelines. If the client is in Dubai, the engine verifies Tier-1 banking requirements. If they are in Zurich, it checks FINMA regulations. Zero hallucination, strict adherence to rules.

STEP 3: Automated CRM & Emails

Based on the analysis, the system updates the client's OTC Readiness Score (0-100). If documents are missing (e.g., unsigned mandate), the Deal Agent automatically sends a branded, context-aware email or Telegram message requesting the exact missing page.

STEP 4: Program of Operations

Once the score hits >90, the system generates a 1-page executive "Program of Operations" report. Deal OTC admins review this standardized dossier and click 'Approve', advancing the client to Active Trading status.

Deal OTC Control & Trust Architecture

Automation does not mean lack of control. The Deal OTC compliance team retains ultimate authority. The AI acts as the Mandate Reviewer—flagging risks, confirming capital thresholds, and preparing the dossier—but the final key is always turned by a human admin.
